Vision

The Global Institute on Innovative Research (GIIR) is geared towards bringing researchers to share their innovative research findings in the global platform

Mission

GIIR's intention is to initiate, develop and promote research in the fields of Social, Economic, Information Technology, Education and Management Sciences

Objective

  • To provide a world class platform for researchers to share their research findings by organizing International and National Conferences.
  • To encourage researchers to identify significant research issues in the identified key areas in Social, Economic, Information Technology, Education and Management.
  • To help in the dissemination of researcher's work through publications in accredited journals and / or conference proceedings or books.
