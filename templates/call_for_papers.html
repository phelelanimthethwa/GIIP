Submission Guidelines

Paper Format

  • Papers must be written in English
  • Maximum length: 8 pages including figures and references
  • Format: PDF
  • Template: IEEE Conference format
  • Font: Times New Roman, 10pt
  • Double column layout

Review Process

  • Double-blind peer review
  • Minimum three reviewers per paper
  • Evaluation based on originality, significance, and clarity
  • Decisions: Accept, Minor Revision, Major Revision, or Reject

Publication

  • Accepted papers will be published in conference proceedings
  • Indexed in major academic databases
  • Selected papers will be invited for journal special issues
  • Copyright form submission required
